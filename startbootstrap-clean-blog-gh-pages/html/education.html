A lil' about me

Here's what I've learned

I've always considered my pursuit of knowledge to be near the top of my list of priorities. Second only to things at the level of importance such as God or family. Although I value the knowledge that a formal education provides, my desire for learning extends far beyond textbooks and classrooms.

My road through university has been rather windy, beginning with me majoring in Neuroscience for a time, before a brief 18 month intermission to serve a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. Upon my return I switched my pursuits to that of an English degree. I thoroughly enjoyed those classes, but ultimately decided Experience Design and Management was the major best suited for what I'm hoping to accomplish in life.

Experience Design and Managament teaches students the creation, facilitation, and management of designing experiences at all levels. I believe experiences change lives for the better. I hope to use this degree to help my children, communities, and corporations build confidence in themselves and their God-given and developed abilities.
